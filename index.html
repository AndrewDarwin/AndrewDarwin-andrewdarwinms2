Hello and welcome to Ibiza Life!

Whether it is golden beaches, the burning sun, gorgeous warm air, beautiful blue waters, live bands, superstar DJs, clubs the size of cathedrals and crowds of tanned party goers from all over the world, Ibiza has it all.

Together with gorgeous coastlines, sunsets, boat trips and hiking opportunities, you have the recipe for one of the most appealing and diverse islands in the world.

An island in the Mediterranean Sea off the eastern coast of Spain, Ibiza is the third largest of the Balearic Islands.

Ibiza is well known for its association with nightlife, electronic dance music and for the summer club scene, all of which attract large numbers of tourists. Although it has some of the best nightlife in the world, Ibiza is a diverse holiday hotspot, and there are plenty of things to do outside of the clubs.

Ibiza has everything you need, beautiful beaches, fantastic food, unforgettable sunsets, water sports, e-biking, guided walks, and much more. We are here to help you find whatever you need, please check out the various things to do below, and contact us on the contact form at the bottom of the page with any requests!

Activities

Sunsets - Ibiza’s beautiful sunsets are simply legendary. The West coast receives the best views, on the sunset strip in San Antonio. You can watch on the rocks, or head to one of the nearby bars or restaurants. Café Del Mar is the most famous of these as it is the original Ibiza sunset café.

Boat trips - Whether it's a party boat, or a more relaxed trip around the island's waters, you'll be able to find lots of different boat trips on the island. At the ferry terminal in Ibiza Town, you can book a boat trip to Formentera - ou'll be greeted with pristine white beaches and crystal clear blue waters.

Hiking - There are some great walking and hiking opportunities on the island. With amazing beautiful coastlines, and beaches, there’s around 250KM to explore! We recommend joining Walking Ibiza, who organise walking tours around the island, whether for leisurely strolls, or more experiences hikes in the hills!

Beaches

Portinatx - The small and quiet resort of Portinatx in the North of the island offers three beautiful beaches - S'Arenal Gros; little S'Arenal Petit and Playa Porto Beach, all with amazing clear waters and white sand.

Cala Llenya - On the East of the island is the beautiful beach of Cala Llenya, which is surrounded by pine trees and excellent turquoise waters. It's a popular beach with families and holiday makers but is never too busy, meaning you'll get an area to yourself even during the peak summers.

Talamanca - Talamanca beach, near Ibiza Town, has a wooden walkway running the length of the beach offering great walks and views. There’s also a good choice of bars and restaurants along the way to stop for some tapas and a beer!

Nightclubs

Amnesia hosts some of the biggest events on the island. In the main room the DJs control the music action from the booth way up high to the masses below, complete with large stacks of loud, powerful speakers and massive ice cannons to keep the clubbers cool throughout the night. The Terrace offers a differing style of music next door.

Privilege is massive - holding 10,000 people it is officially the biggest club in the world. The venue is stunning, set in a huge dome with various gardens, and back rooms. The setting for the main room is a grand DJ stage, with Privilege’s world famous cinema screen behind the DJ booth blurring out colourful visuals alongside the DJ sets.

Ushuaïa is an outside hotel and club venue in playa d'en Bossa set in beautiful surroundings. Offering amazing production, visuals and line ups, complete with the warm Ibiza sun beating down, and planes flying in overhead to the nearby airport, and you have the best outside clubbing experience in the world.
